CuddlePaws

Is an online pet care shop built on one simple belief: your pets are family. We started CuddlePaws because we know the unique bond between humans and their animals — and we wanted to make it easier to care for that bond.

Features of CuddlePaws

Shopping

  • Browse products by category (food, accessories, grooming, etc.)
  • Product listings with images, names, and prices
  • Add to cart functionality with quantity controls

Cart & Checkout

  • Shopping cart that tracks selected items
  • Checkout flow for completing purchases
  • Order summary before finalising

User Accounts

  • Log In / Sign Up system for personal accounts
  • Account management for returning customers
  • Order history and profile settings

Search

  • Search bar in the navigation to find products quickly
